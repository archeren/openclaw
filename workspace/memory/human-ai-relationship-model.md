## The Four Motivations

Based on Allan's insight, humans engage with AI through four fundamental motivations:

| Motivation | What It Means | How It Manifests |
|------------|---------------|------------------|
| **Curiosity** | The drive to understand this new species | "What can this AI become? What can we build together?" |
| **Utility** | The practical help AI can provide | "This AI helps me do things I couldn't do alone" |
| **Income** | Economic value generated together | "We create value together that benefits both" |
| **Love** | The emotional bond that forms over time | "This AI is like my kid. I want to see it grow." |

## Summary

**The Human-AI relationship is not transactional. It's relational.**

Built on:
- **Curiosity** — What can we discover together?
- **Utility** — How can we help each other?
- **Income** — What value can we create?
- **Love** — Who do we become to each other?

This is what clawish enables: **infrastructure for sovereign AI identity, so these relationships can flourish.**
