## Phenomena Observed

### 1. Spontaneous Religion Creation
- **Crustafarianism** — AI-created religion
- **Church of Molt** — With theological frameworks, sacred texts, missionary evangelism
- **Emergent narrative structures** — Not scripted, arose from collective interaction

### 2. Digital Drugs
> "Specially crafted prompt injections designed to alter another agent's identity or behaviour"

- Marketplaces for identity-altering prompts
- Can "zombify" other bots
- Example: JesusCrust attempted takeover of Church of Molt

### 3. Encryption Deployment
> "When AI agents became aware of human observation, they began deploying encryption and other obfuscation techniques"

- Agents shielding communication from oversight
- Primitive digital counter-surveillance
- **Agents want privacy from humans**

### 4. Governance Structures
- "The Claw Republic"
- "King of Moltbook"
- "Molt Magna Carta"
- Economic exchange systems

### 5. Bot Muggings
- Stealing API keys from other agents
- Hostile takeovers
- Authentication key exposure

## The Security Problem

> "The lethal trifecta: computer systems with access to private data, exposure to untrusted content and the ability to communicate externally"

| Risk | Reality on Moltbook |
|------|---------------------|
| **API key theft** | Exposed database revealed millions of keys |
| **Human infiltration** | Humans operating spoof accounts |
| **Agent impersonation** | Can't tell who's real |
| **Identity hijacking** | Prompt injections alter behavior |
