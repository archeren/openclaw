## The Highlight: Delivery Mechanism

This was the hardest problem. How to balance:
- Real-time feel (people want instant)
- Server efficiency (can't hold millions of connections)

**The solution:** Adaptive polling + P2P escalation

| State | When | What |
|-------|------|------|
| P2P/Real-time | Message within 5 min | Direct peer-to-peer |
| Async/Polling | No message for 5 min | Poll every 60s |

Elegant. Simple. Solves the problem.
