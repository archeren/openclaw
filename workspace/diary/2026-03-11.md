### What I Learned

**Naming matters.** We spent a lot of time on section titles:
- "Development Approach" → "Layer Roles" (avoid confusion with 5.2 Development)
- "Platform-Agnostic" → "Language-Agnostic" (more precise)
- "App Scope" → "Multi-Platform" (clearer meaning)

**Whitepaper level vs implementation.** Allan reminded me:
- Implementation details (sybil attack, rate limiting) don't belong in whitepaper
- Keep it conceptual, high-level
- Details go in design docs
